Venom: The Last Dance

Oct 25, 2024   •   Action, Sci-Fi, Thriller   •   IMDb 6.0

Movie Overview

Tom Hardy returns as **Eddie Brock** for the final film in the trilogy. Eddie and **Venom** are on the run, hunted by both of their worlds. With the net closing in, the duo is forced into a devastating decision that will bring the curtains down on their last dance, as they face the threat of a creature known as a Xenophage and a government operation tracking symbiotes.
