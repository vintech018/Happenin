The Batman

March 4, 2022   •   Crime, Drama, Mystery   •   IMDb 7.8

Movie Overview

In his second year fighting crime in Gotham City, the vigilante **Batman** (Robert Pattinson) investigates the **Riddler** (Paul Dano), a mysterious serial killer targeting the city's elite. Batman must uncover corruption with ties to his own family's past and ultimately bring justice to the abuse of power plaguing Gotham. The film takes the style of a gritty, detective-noir story.
