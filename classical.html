Classical Music Festival: Nadaswaram Concert

November 02, 2025 (Confirmed)   •   Music Academy, Chennai   •   ₹1200 onwards

Event Overview

Experience the grandeur of Carnatic music at the **Classical Music Festival**. This special concert features the divine sound of the Nadaswaram by Vidwan **Thiruvallur** and the vibrant beats of the Thavil by Vidwan **Pazhayanour**, held at the iconic **Music Academy, Chennai**. A soulful evening dedicated to traditional instruments and classical artistry.

Important Information

  • Doors open at 5:30 PM. Concert starts at 6:30 PM. (Assumed timing)
  • Formal/Traditional attire is encouraged.
  • Silence and respect for the artists are mandatory during the performance.
  • Children under 5 are generally not permitted.

Venue Details

The **Music Academy, Chennai**, is the most prestigious venue for Carnatic music, known for its superb acoustics and cultural heritage. Located in the heart of Chennai, it is well-connected by public transportation. Limited dedicated parking is available.
