Coldplay World Tour

October 15, 2025 (Confirmed)   •   Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium, Delhi   •   ₹3500 onwards

Event Overview

The globally renowned **Coldplay World Tour** is finally hitting Delhi! Prepare for a spectacular night featuring massive hits like "Fix You," "Viva la Vida," and "A Sky Full of Stars." This is a once-in-a-lifetime chance to experience the band's famous pyrotechnics and stunning light show live at the **Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium**.

Important Information

  • Gates open at 5:00 PM. Concert starts at 7:30 PM.
  • E-tickets must be exchanged for physical wristbands at the venue.
  • Use of professional cameras and recording equipment is strictly prohibited.
  • Children below 6 years are not permitted.

Venue Details

The **Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium** is one of India's largest concert venues, offering ample seating and general access space. It is easily accessible via the Delhi Metro. Please plan to arrive early to account for security checks and large crowds.
