Movie Overview

The sequel to Joker (2019) finds Arthur Fleck institutionalized at Arkham State Hospital, where he meets Harleen Quinzel (Lady Gaga). While struggling with his dual identity, Arthur stumbles upon true love and finds the music that's always been inside him, leading to a vibrant, imaginative jukebox musical.
